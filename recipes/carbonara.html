Carbonara Recipe

Description

Just the magnificent flavour of eggs, cheese and bacon on an awesome pasta, one of the most tradicional recipes from Italia, also one of the easiest. Great flavours with less ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Eggs
  • Bacon
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. chop the bacon, cook to make it crispy and reserve
  2. crack half of the eggs and separate yolk from white
  3. crack the other half of the eggs and put all the egg, without separating
  4. beat those eggs with the chesse, pepper and salt. Reserve the mix
  5. Put the water to boil
  6. Add 1 spon of salt for each 1l. of water after boiling
  7. Add pasta to te pan
  8. Pickup 1 cup off the heated water from the cooking pasta
  9. After take the pasta from water, put the bacon on the pan
  10. Add the pasta over the bacon, add the eggs and pour the water from pasta cooking
  11. Mix all the ingredients on low fire
  12. Serve on plate
