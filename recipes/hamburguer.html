Hamburguer Recipe

Description

The most famous recipes on Brazil, that we expect every weekend, the brazilian barbecue it's just awesome, ain't just food or a recipe, it's a moment, when we reunite our friends, drink some beers and do some chitty chats. Picanha's it's one of the most desired cuts from Brazilian BBQ, they have a thick layer of fat that gives the protein a marvelous flavour.

Ingredients

  • Red meat (Picanha, ribs, neck meat)
  • Chicken
  • Sausages
  • Chicken heart
  • Garlic bread

Steps

  1. Light the charcoal
  2. Put the meat on barbecue
  3. Turn the side
  4. Take from fire and let rest for 3 min
  5. Cut with a sharp knife
  6. Put some flower of salt
  7. Be happy
